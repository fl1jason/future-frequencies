Every month, we spend time digging through the underground to find the tracks that capture the essence of what we're trying to create. These are the artists and producers who are pushing deep house forward, who are finding new sounds while respecting the foundation.

June brought some exceptional discoveries. We found a London-based producer who's blending analog synthesis with field recordings from nature. Another artist from Barcelona is exploring the intersection of house and ambient music, creating tracks that work on the dance floor and in contemplative spaces.

What excites us about these artists is their intentionality. They're not chasing trends or algorithms. They're making music that speaks to their artistic vision, and in doing so, they're creating the soundtrack for communities that appreciate authentic expression.

Supporting these artists is crucial. When you hear an underground gem at one of our events, you're hearing someone's creative vision. You're supporting an artist who might never have mainstream recognition but who is creating music that moves people on a deep level.

We believe that part of our responsibility as curators is to amplify these voices, to help these artists reach the communities that will appreciate their work. This is how underground music stays alive and vital—through genuine appreciation and active support.

If you discover an underground artist whose work resonates with you, support them directly. Buy their music, go to their events, share their work with others who might appreciate it.
